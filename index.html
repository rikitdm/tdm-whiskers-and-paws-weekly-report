Key Insights

  • Organic Search Remains a Pillar: This channel continues to be a strong and efficient driver of high-quality customers, with a healthy increase in its conversion rate.

Insights & Recommendations

Actionable steps to build on momentum and address opportunities.

1. Launch a Targeted Subscription Campaign

Design and launch a dedicated marketing campaign for the "Food" customer segment, highlighting the convenience and value of subscribing to convert them from one-off purchasers.

2. Optimize Our Paid Search Strategy

Initiate a comprehensive review of top-performing and highest-spending paid search campaigns. Analyze keywords, ad copy, and landing pages to fix the conversion rate dip.

3. Double Down on What's Working in Organic Search

Continue investing in SEO strategy. Prioritize creating content and optimizing pages around high-intent keywords to capture more of this ready-to-buy audience.

4. Refine Our Email Marketing Cadence

Analyze the elements of the top-performing campaign ("Hongkong Dimsum Moment") and test applying these insights to underperforming campaigns to create a playbook for driving revenue through email.
